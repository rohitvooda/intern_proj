# Pitch Deck Slide Outline - AI ScienceVerse

This document contains the presentation slide mapping for showcasing **AI ScienceVerse** at the AI Model Development Contest 2026.

---

## Slide 1: Title & Hook
- **Slide Title**: AI ScienceVerse: STEM Education Reimagined
- **Subtitle**: Gamified 3D Explorers, Real-Time Physics Simulations, and Adaptive AI Tutoring
- **Visuals**: High-resolution screenshot of the 3D Biology Cell Explorer and glowing cosmos background.
- **Key Message**: Active, visual, and gamified learning is the future of science education.

---

## Slide 2: The Problem
- **Slide Title**: The Crisis in STEM Engagement
- **Key Points**:
  - **Static Textbooks**: Abstract diagrams fail to capture spatial relations (e.g. cell organelle volume).
  - **Passive Learning**: Memorizing mechanics equations without seeing cause-and-effect.
  - **One-Size-Fits-All**: Classroom curriculum cannot adapt explanations to different age levels or study paces.
- **Visuals**: A stock image of a bored student looking at a dry textbook vs. a question mark icon.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution
- **Slide Title**: Introducing AI ScienceVerse
- **Key Pillars**:
  - **Interactive 3D Visualizations**: Spatial comprehension of cell biology using React Three Fiber.
  - **Dynamic Physics Simulations**: Real-time variables (gravity, angles) and live graph telemetry.
  - **Personalized AI Tutor**: Adaptive path scheduling, weakness detection, and multi-tier age explanations.
  - **Gamification**: XP tracking, streaks, and achievement badges to incentivize daily study.

---

## Slide 4: Technology Architecture
- **Slide Title**: Production-Ready Tech Stack
- **Bullet Points**:
  - **Frontend**: Next.js 15, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Zustand, Recharts, and Three.js (React Three Fiber).
  - **Backend**: FastAPI (Python), SQLAlchemy, JWT Authentication, and Pydantic validation.
  - **Database & AI**: Supabase PostgreSQL database; OpenAI & Gemini API integration with offline mock fallback.
  - **Deployment**: Vercel (Client App) and Render (Server API).

---

## Slide 5: Feature Walkthrough: Biology & Physics
- **Slide Title**: Immersive STEM Labs
- **Key Points**:
  - **3D Cell Lab**: Click organelles to reveal facts, ask AI for kid-friendly analogies, or read explanations aloud.
  - **Mechanics Lab**: Live projectile and pendulum simulation, real-time kinetic calculations, and live graph plotting.
- **Visuals**: Dual mockups showing the 3D cell and the Recharts parameter graph.

---

## Slide 6: Adaptive AI & Gamification Loop
- **Slide Title**: The Personalized Learning Engine
- **Key Points**:
  - **AI Learning Paths**: Generates specific steps based on student achievements.
  - **Weakness Analysis**: Automatically tracks failed quiz questions to create custom flashcards and study notes.
  - **Gamified Rewards**: Level up and earn badges like "Biology Genius" or "Code Architect".

---

## Slide 7: Market Potential & Future Roadmaps
- **Slide Title**: Future Vision
- **Key Milestones**:
  - **Chemistry Lab Module**: 3D atomic bond visualizer and molecular reactions.
  - **Teacher Dashboard**: Let educators assign custom quiz files and track classroom weaknesses.
  - **Voice Commands**: Let students navigate simulations completely hands-free using voice.
